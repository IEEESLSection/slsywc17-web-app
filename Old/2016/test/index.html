IEEE Sri Lanka Section Student/YP/WIE Congress '16

The IEEE Sri Lanka Section, for the fifth consecutive year, is getting ready to present the Sri Lankan IEEE enthusiasts with yet another memorable episode of the Sri Lanka Section Student / Young Professional / Women in Engineering Congress for the year of 2016. This is undoubtedly the most awaited event of the IEEE Sri Lankan section where Student Branches,Young Professionals and Women in Engineering share their achievements to receive public endorsement and inspire others.

Starting in 2012, it has been a long journey in bringing the congress to the stratum it now enjoys and the section expects to reap more and more with each year.

This year the congress is themed on “EXPANDING HORIZONS" and to facilitate the theme, SLSYWC16 will span over three days for the first time in its history.

The congress will commence on the 25th of November and will continue till 27th of November 2016. The days will be filled with inspirational and informational speeches by wonderful personalities, fun games, entertainment and even surprises!

Student

The student members of the Sri Lanka section are currently operating under nine Student Branches and more are taking initiatives to establish their own Student Branches. The number of student members making their presence in the IEEE community keep piling up everyday.

Young Professional

The Sri Lankan Young Professional community is rich with passionate Engineers, Scientists and Technical Experts who love what they do. They are intent on doing what’s best for the community and themselves.

Women In Engineering

A community of women and enthusiastic men that takes initiative in bringing out the passion for technology in women. They conduct lots of workshops and gatherings for the benefit of women in technology. Young students are also operating as a part of this affinity group under their respective Student Branches.
