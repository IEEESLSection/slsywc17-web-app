IEEE Sri Lanka Section Student/YP/WIE Congress '16

Awards Ceremony

For the first time in congress history,IEEE Sri Lanka section is organizing an Awards Night to recognize the most outstanding Volunteers and Student Branches in the Sri Lanka Section. Considering the most tremendous growth of enthusiasm of the volunteers, the section decided to present following awards for activities for the year 2016.




Best Student Branch Activity Award

This award goes to the best activity organized by a Student Branch that made the biggest positive impact on the student branch members in the last calendar year, also weighing in factors of team spirit, camaraderie and volunteerism among the members.


Best Young Professional Activity Award

This award goes to the most outstanding initiative taken by the young professionals community that proved to be of strategic importance to increase the quality and add value to the young professionals in the island.




Best WIE Activity Award

The award goes to the most outstanding initiative taken by a Women in Engineering affinity group of IEEE WIE that proved to be engaged in increasing the interests of women in engineering disciplines while enhancing their technological and professional skills.Empowering and fostering leadership skills and carrier advancement also caters for the eligibility.
