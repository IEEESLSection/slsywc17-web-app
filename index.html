IEEE Sri Lanka Section Student/YP/WIE Congress '17

The IEEE Sri Lanka Section, for the sixth consecutive year, is getting ready to present the Sri Lankan IEEE enthusiasts with yet another memorable episode of the Sri Lanka Section Student / Young Professional / Women in Engineering Congress for the year of 2017. This is undoubtedly the most awaited event of the IEEE Sri Lanka Section where Student Branches,Young Professionals and Women in Engineering share their achievements to receive public endorsement and inspire others.

Starting in 2012, it has been a long journey in bringing the congress to the stratum it enjoys today and the section expects to reap more and more with each year.

This year the congress is themed on “VISION BEYOND TECHNOLOGY” and IEEE SLSYWC’17 will consist of various interesting sessions to facilitate the theme.

Student

The student members of IEEE Sri Lanka Section are currently operating under thirteen Student Branches and more are taking initiatives to establish their own Student Branch. The number of student members making their presence in the IEEE community keep piling up everyday.

Young Professional

The Sri Lankan Young Professional community is rich with passionate Engineers, Scientists and Technical Experts who love what they do. They are intent on doing what’s best for the community and themselves.

Women In Engineering

A community of women and enthusiastic men that takes initiative in bringing out the passion for technology in women. They conduct lots of workshops and gatherings for the benefit of women in technology. Young students are also operating as a part of this affinity group under their respective Student Branches.

IEEE SLSYWC 2016

Last year the congress was themed on “EXPANDING HORIZONS" and to facilitate the theme, it spanned over three days for the first time in its history.

The congress will span from 22nd of December to 24th of December 2017. The days will be filled with inspirational and informational speeches by wonderful personalities, fun games and contests, entertainment and even surprises!

Venue

University of Sabaragamuwa

The proud host for this year’s Congress is University of Sabaragamuwa. The University premises are rich with plenty of modern facilities to entertain the delegates.
